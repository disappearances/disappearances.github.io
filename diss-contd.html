Reports of Other Mechanisms

The material on this page relates to reports of other state appointed mechanisms into disappearances. It also includes reports of Commissions of Inquiry that did not exclusively look into disappearances but where disappearances were included in the mandate (Udalagama Commission and LLRC).

HRC Committee on Disappearances

The Human Rights Commission appointed the Committee to look into disappearances from Jaffna, Kilinochchi, and Vavuniya. The Chair was Devenesan Nesiah. The report is dated October 2003.

Udalagama Commissions

In November 2006, President Rajapakshe appointed a Presidential Commission of Inquiry to look into 16 cases of serious violations of human rights. The Chair was Nissanka Kumara Udalagama. An International Independent Group of Eminent Persons (IIGEP) was appointed to observe the investigations and inquiries of the Commission.

LLRC

In May 2010, President Rajapaksa, appointed a Presidential Commission of Inquiry on Lessons Learnt and Reconciliation (LLRC). The Chair was Chitta Ranjan de Silva. The final report is dated November 2011.
